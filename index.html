Digital Forensic Solutions

Our forensic technology platforms are designed to support evidentiary examination across audio, video, digital, and transportation domains. Each solution addresses a specific stage of the forensic workflow - from authenticity verification and signal enhancement to source identification and analytical reporting - and may be deployed independently or as part of an integrated investigative methodology.

Forensic Video Authentication & Source Identification

When video recordings are presented as legal evidence, their integrity must be technically validated before they can support investigative or judicial decisions.

This solution provides a comprehensive set of forensic analysis tools designed to authenticate video recordings, detect alterations in the sequence of events or recorded content, and identify the originating recording device - supporting experts throughout the evidentiary examination process.

Developed in collaboration with forensic laboratories and practitioners during real casework, the platform applies a multi-layered methodology to assess video authenticity.

  • Recording device memory examination
  • File header and structural consistency analysis
  • Intra-frame image integrity assessment
  • Inter-frame continuity verification
  • Audio-video stream synchronization analysis
  • Source device identification

Forensic Image & Video Enhancement

This solution is designed for forensic examination of digital images and video recordings to improve visual clarity and enable the identification of critical evidentiary details related to events captured at crime scenes.

Developed in cooperation with forensic practitioners during real investigations, the platform applies both traditional and advanced enhancement methodologies while preserving evidentiary integrity.

Forensic Audio Authentication & Speaker Identification

This solution provides forensic experts with advanced tools for authentication of audio evidence, speech enhancement for intelligibility improvement, structured conversation segmentation, and forensic speaker voice identification - supporting reliable interpretation of recorded conversations used in investigations.

Developed in collaboration with practicing forensic audio specialists during real case examinations, the platform supports the full workflow of speech recording analysis:

  • Speech signal enhancement and processing
  • Conversation segmentation with text decoding
  • Auditory and linguistic analysis
  • Prosodic speech pattern assessment
  • Spectral and phonetic profiling
  • Pitch and formant statistical analysis

AI-Powered Forensic Audio Analysis & Authenticity Assessment

This solution supports forensic audio experts by automating time-consuming analytical tasks using advanced artificial intelligence and signal processing algorithms - enabling efficient speaker segmentation, speech decoding, and text-independent speaker identification.

In addition to identity comparison, the platform provides automated tools for speaker profiling and digital audio authenticity assessment, including detection of analog re-recordings and synthetic voices generated through voice cloning or deepfake technologies.

  • Automatic speaker segmentation and labeling
  • Text-independent speaker identification
  • Speaker profiling (age / gender estimation)
  • Detection of analog channel re-recordings
  • Synthetic speech and deepfake voice detection
  • Likelihood Ratio (LR / LLR) statistical analysis

Graphical Probability Density Function plots and FA / FR rate curves support expert interpretation of similarity degree, decision thresholds, and evidential strength in support of investigative hypotheses.

Forensic Video-Based Scene Reconstruction & Motion Analysis

This solution is designed to support forensic investigation of incidents captured on video recordings, including traffic accidents and other events requiring spatial and temporal reconstruction of the scene.

Using geometric modeling and motion analysis techniques, the platform enables experts to establish critical evidentiary parameters such as distance, time intervals, and object speed directly from video evidence.

  • Multi-format video evidence playback
  • Optical distortion compensation
  • Distance and spatial measurement
  • Trajectory reconstruction of moving objects
  • Object speed calculation
  • 3D modeling of the recorded scene

Professional Training & Methodological Support

All supplied forensic technology platforms are delivered together with professional training programs as an essential part of system implementation. Due to the technical complexity of modern forensic analysis tools, proper methodological application of software functions is critical to ensure evidentiary reliability and the correctness of expert conclusions.

Our primary objective is not only to provide analytical instruments, but to ensure their competent and consistent use within real investigative workflows. For this reason, training is considered a mandatory component accompanying the deployment of each solution.

Training programs are traditionally conducted on-site at the customer’s premises to allow direct interaction with operational specialists and laboratory environments. Following the COVID-19 pandemic, remote training formats were additionally introduced and may be provided online when necessary, although in-person instruction remains the recommended format for optimal knowledge transfer.

Our certified trainers have conducted more than 100 specialized training courses worldwide for forensic experts, law enforcement agencies, and judicial institutions.

Post-training methodological support is maintained through ongoing expert consultation. Our specialists remain in contact with trained personnel and provide advisory assistance regarding practical application of analytical techniques that may arise during real case examinations.
